Explore South Africa Site Plan

Site Name

Explore South Africa

This name was chosen because the site highlights South Africa's natural beauty, cultural richness, and modern achivements. It is simple, memorable and directly communicates the purpose of the site.

Optional domain: exploresouthafrica.org

Site Purpose

The site will showcase destinations, culture, and achivements of South Africa. It will provide real-time weather updates, destination guides and a contact form for the user engagement. The purpose is to promote tourism, investment, and appreciation of South Africa whil demonstrating frontend development skills.

Scenarios

  • Which destinations should I visit in South Africa?
  • What is the current weather in Cape Town, Johannesburg, and Pretoria?
  • How can I suggest a hidden gem or ask for travel tips?

Color Scheme

Primary color: #006400

Accent color: #FFD700

Background color: #f9f9f9

Typography

Heading Font: Montserrat - clean and modern, used for titles and navigation.

Body Font: Roboto - easy to read, used for paragraphs and descriptions.

Project Structure

  • Home Page (index.html): Hero image, intro text, responsive navigation, weather widget.
  • Destination Page: Dynamic cards (15+ places) fetched from local JSON, with modal dialogs and maps.
  • Contact Page: HTML form for suggestions and travel tips, with action page to display submitted data.
