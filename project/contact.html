Frequently Asked Questions

South Africa is a year-round destination. The best time depends on your interests: September-November for spring flowers, December-February for summer beach weather, and May-August for safari viewing (dry season with better wildlife visibility).

Visa requirements vary by nationality. Many countries (including USA, UK, EU, Australia, Canada) don't need a visa for stays up to 90 days. Always check current requirements with the South African Department of Home Affairs before traveling.

South Africa is generally safe for tourists who take normal precautions. We recommend staying in well-traveled areas, not displaying valuables, using reputable tour operators, and following local advice. Our team provides specific safety tips for each destination.

We can help you plan the perfect safari! Popular options include Kruger National Park, Sabi Sands, and Addo Elephant Park. Consider time of year, budget, accommodation type (luxury lodges vs camping), and whether you want a guided or self-drive safari.
